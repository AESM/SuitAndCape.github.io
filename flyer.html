Allow me to introduce myself...

I aim to create intuitive and minimalistic software.
I build, I fiddle, and I embrace my playful curiosity – all of which help me to solve problems I have never encountered before. There will always be a better alternative to boredom, and I endorse creativity and the pursuit of knowledge as reliable substitutes.
I love having the freedom to choose the right tool for the job and the opportunity to expand my knowledge.

We need to go deeper.

I'm deeply and intensely curious about the "why," which is where my love for learning new skills and tools in a quick and effective manner stems from.
One thing that I find inspiring and exciting is when something or someone has the ability to level the playing field – I'm especially interested in contributing to products and services that make high-quality education more accessible or that facilitate open communication and virtual community-building.
Other interests include the film and video-game industries, physical sciences, fashion, design, and culinary arts. If I can feel the passion behind it, it can probably catch my interest.

Oh, the places you'll go!

I love to build. I love to learn. I love to share. I love being a developer.
I've worked on mobile apps, modular back-ends, and responsive websites; consistently, the most rewarding part of the development process is working on a product that people will not only want to use, but will also benefit from and enjoy using.
My tech career began at Zvents, an event promotion website with a heavy emphasis on local groups businesses. I managed thousands of event listings across the nation manually, through the site's CMS, and by monitoring automated iMacros.
When the company was acquired by eBay, I was asked to stay on board and soon became a part of the StubHub family. There, I maintained and enhanced the quality of content for events and venues appearing in StubHub’s music-tour app for Spotify andconcert app for the iPhone, StubHub Music. I was appointed as project manager for three key projects that focused on internal content standards and task delegation. Later, I assisted with various ad impressions and advertising projects.
After I fully realized my passion to code, I began teaching myself more technical programming skills. Shortly thereafter, I applied for and was admitted into the 19-week immersive coding school, Dev Bootcamp...
