  • Oh, the places you'll go!

    I've worked on mobile apps, modular back-ends, and responsive websites. I'm always excited when I'm working on a product that people will not only want to use, but will benefit from and enjoy using.

    I am a graduate of Dev Bootcamp, a 19-week immersive coding program based in San Francisco. During my training, I honed my coding skills in Ruby and JavaScript, while practicing Agile development and mobile-first design. I also grew adept at learning how to learn: selecting the best technologies for a project and quickly becoming proficient with them.

    Before that, I worked at Zvents, an event promotion startup. I managed thousands of event listings across the nation, through the site's CMS and by monitoring automated iMacros. When the company was acquired by eBay/StubHub, I joined their content team. There, I worked on content for StubHub’s iPhone and Spotify apps. I was appointed as project manager for three key projects that focused on internal content standards and task delegation. It was during this time that I began to teach myself coding and became totally (and happily!) consumed by the programming gods.
